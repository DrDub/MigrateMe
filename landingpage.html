Welcome to Quaquaversal.
Quaquaversal is a site that brings together different stories and people from around the world in one place.
It is a space to reflect on the modern phenomenon of continuous movement, migration, and travel of life. All too often as we move through countries, continents and time zones we forget to share our experiences as a dialogues with others. Take a look around, read the resources and, if you like, tell us your story.

Not all those who wander are lost. JRR Tolkien
